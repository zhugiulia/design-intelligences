TOWARDS DESIGN INTELLIGENCES


The Design Intelligences Institute is a collective of humans and nonhumans, bringing together diverse forms of life and intelligence to address the complex environmental and societal challenges our planet faces.

Our mission is to engage in more-than-human design research and practices that integrate human, natural, and artificial ways of knowing and world making.

We refer to this synergistic integration with the notion of “design intelligences” .
