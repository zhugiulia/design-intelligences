A MORE-THAN-HUMAN COLLECTIVE

We are a more-than-human collective of designers, researchers, thinkers, and makers reimagining how design can foster regenerative lifestyles. Our community spans faculty, PhD candidates, graduation lab students, research apprentices, and visiting fellows. We collaborate with nonhuman agents - plants, animals, microbes, and AI - as co-thinkers and co-creators. From alumni to current members, each brings unique insights and practices that contribute to a shared mission.
