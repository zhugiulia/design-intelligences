Open Grounds

Co-Thinkers Welcome!
Open Grounds is where new collaborations take root. This page gathers opportunities for graduation theses, research internships, visiting scholars, and artists-in-residence who wish to engage with more-than-human perspectives in design. It is also a space for forming partnerships with research groups, institutions, cultural organizations, and design studios committed to rethinking human-nonhuman relations.

Research Apprenticeship

Are you looking for a research apprenticeship or internship experience within an interdisciplinary design research lab? We welcome spontaneous applications from students who are eager to engage with our ongoing projects or explore new areas of inquiry within our themes.

Opportunities for Academics

Junior Visiting Positions

We welcome expressions of interest from early-career researchers, postdoctoral fellows, and junior faculty wishing to spend a period of time at our lab. Visiting scholars may take part in collaborative projects, initiate new ones, or contribute to teaching and mentoring students.

mail
Senior Visiting Positions

Senior researchers and professors are invited to engage with our lab as visiting fellows. We are open to co-developing research agendas, organizing symposia or workshops, and creating synergies across institutions through short- or long-term stays.

mail

Collaborations

Let's talk

We welcome collaborations with research groups, institutions, cultural organizations, and design studios that share our interest in more-than-human perspectives and ecosystemic change. Whether you're developing a project, curating a program, or exploring speculative futures, we’d love to hear from you. Let’s shape meaningful futures together.
