Mattering

This is a living archive of experiments, theories, and alliances between humans, technologies, and other forms of intelligence. Here you’ll find a growing body of work that spans research projects, peer-reviewed publications, design studios, and experimental pedagogies. From speculative prototypes to scholarly reflections, this evolving collection captures the ongoing effort to shift design from a human-centered practice to one rooted in multispecies collaboration and planetary care.
